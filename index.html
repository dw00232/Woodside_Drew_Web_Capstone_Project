Welcome Gamer

Our purpose is to Provide a dedicated space for honest, community-driven video game reviews and news while bridging the gap between indie developers and casual gamers.

Goals:

  • Build trust through unbiased, detailed game reviews.
  • Help indie developers gain traction for their game.
  • Create an engaging space for users.

Target Audience:

People aged 18-40 of all genders, who are looking for honest opinions on lesser-known games before purchasing.
